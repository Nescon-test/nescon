Investments

Learn More
Keppel REIT
Keppel Land is the sponsor of Keppel REIT and holds a significant interest in the REIT. Keppel REIT is one of Asia's leading REITs with a portfolio of premium Grade A commercial assets in prime business and financial districts pan‐Asia. It is managed by Keppel REIT Management Limited, a wholly‐owned subsidiary of Keppel Capital Holdings Pte. Ltd. (Keppel Capital). Keppel Capital is a premier asset manager in Asia with a diversified portfolio in real estate, infrastructure and data centre properties in key global markets.
Learn More
Alpha Investment Partners
Keppel Land is invested in several funds managed by Alpha Investment Partners (Alpha). Alpha is a private fund investment manager that aims to add value for investors by seeking innovative solutions throughout its investment process. Its private funds are invested in different asset classes including real estate and data centres, and across the risk spectrum in the core, core-plus, value-add and opportunistic space. Alpha is a wholly-owned subsidiary of Keppel Capital.
Learn More
Keppel Philippines Properties Inc
Keppel Philippines Properties Inc (KPPI) has been listed on the Philippines Stock Exchange since 1989. It is majority owned by Keppel Land and Keppel Corporation. KPPI undertakes property development activities through its associates and joint venture and has successfully completed residential and commercial developments in The Philippines, including, in Manila, Palmdale Heights, a residential development located in Pasig City, and The Podium Complex, a mixed-use development located in the heart of Ortigas’ CBD.
Learn More
Smartworks
Keppel Land has invested in Smartworks, a leading pan-India flexible space solutions provider. Smartworks provides quality flexible spaces primarily to large MNC enterprises and established companies, with multiple locations in Bangalore, Delhi NCR, Mumbai, Pune, Hyderabad, Chennai, and Kolkata.
Learn More
Fifth Wall
Keppel is a strategic limited partner in Fifth Wall Ventures Fund II via Keppel Ventures. Fifth Wall is one of the world's largest built world venture capital funds, with Fund II having raised more than >US$500m from strategic international real estate companies and top-tier investment funds.
Learn More
COVE
Keppel Land is a strategic and lead investor in Cove’s Series A funding round. Cove provides convenient and hassle-free accommodation solutions to young professionals and students and leverages technology to provide for a seamless user experience. Founded in 2018, Cove is one of Southeast Asia’s fastest growing co-living companies.
