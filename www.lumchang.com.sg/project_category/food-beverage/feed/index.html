]]> Black Tap Craft Burgers & Beer https://www.lumchang.com.sg/projects_uri/black-tap-craft-burgers-beer/ Thu, 27 Sep 2018 09:06:43 +0000 https://www.lumchang.com.sg/?post_type=projects_uri&p=704 Interior works of Black Tap Craft Burgers & Beer’s first Asian outlet at Marina Bay Sands (MBS). The New York-based restaurant founded by Michelin-starred chef, Joe Isidori, is designed by the Rockwell Group.

The scope of works include interior fitting-out, M&E and structural works. The more than 150-seater restaurant, will take diners back in time with the casual, classic American luncheonette design, jazzed up with ’80s and ’90s hip hop grooves and splashes of colour.

The project was completed in the third quarter of 2018.
