Hear From Our Keppelites

Sok wei - Digital & Technology

Opportunities are endless in Keppel with the diversity in our business. I took up a different role in Keppel Land contributing directly to the sustainable urbanisation business after a wonderful 5-year journey with the Group’s Shared Services driving enterprise IT transformative initiatives. It’s exhilarating to be part of an amazing team working with like-minded people, knowing that we’re making an impact towards a greener and more sustainable future together!

Jermaine - New Ventures

I’ve been given opportunities to learn and execute in close proximity to senior management, developing my career in ways beyond my initial expectations. The management trusts their teams with the appropriate space to ideate and experiment. I was given the chance to work on corporate innovation, lead an agile team of empassioned peers, and work in the venture space to build company capabilities in proptech. The opportunity to work on things I am passionate about has made work extremely fulfilling and rewarding.

Joel - Associate Program Graduate

Working in Keppel Land can be described with one word, "opportunity". I’m constantly inspired to embrace challenges, acquire new skills, and explore new ways of thinking in this innovative, collaborative and agile culture. An enriching and supportive environment also provides the necessary guidance and platforms to stretch, learn, and grow both professionally and personally.
