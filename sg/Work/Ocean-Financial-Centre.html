Specifications

Developed by Keppel Land, Ocean Financial Centre is a 43-storey premium Grade A office tower situated at the heart of Singapore’s financial belt. It is located at the intersection of the Raffles Place and Marina Bay precincts, offering over 876,000 sf of premium office space with column-free floor plates ranging between 20,000 sf and 23,000 sf. It is connected by a thoroughfare that links Marina Bay precincts to Raffles Place MRT interchange. The office tower also offers various dining options and amenities on the ground floor and basement levels.

In 2019, Ocean Financial Centre was once again recognised for its sustainable design and features with the Green Mark Platinum Award recertification by the Building and Construction Authority of Singapore.

Location

10 Collyer Quay Singapore 049315

Situated at the intersection of the Central Business District (CBD) and new Downtown at Marina Bay, the peerless location of Ocean Financial Centre makes it the perfect place for work and play.

Privy to a myriad of entertainment outlets, this world-class development is a stone’s throw away from Singapore’s vibrant cultural hub, Esplanade – Theatres on the Bay and Marina Bay Sands.

For frequent business travelers, Changi International Airport is a mere 20-minute drive away. The building is directly linked to Raffles Place MRT Station, a key interchange station for the East-West Line and North-South Line, as well as Downtown MRT Station.

At Ocean Financial Centre, business and leisure blend seamlessly together in the heart of Singapore’s financial and business district.
