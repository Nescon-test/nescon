We deliver innovative urban space solutions that enrich people and communities

Keppel Land is a wholly-owned subsidiary of Keppel Corporation, a Singapore flagship multinational company providing solutions for sustainable urbanisation, focusing on energy & environment, urban development, connectivity and asset management.

Keppel Land is an innovative urban space solutions provider that leverages technology to deliver sustainable and customer-centric solutions that enrich people and communities. It is focused in the areas of sustainable urban renewal, senior living, urban living, retail and large-scale integrated developments.    

Its award-winning portfolio comprises investment-grade commercial properties, residential developments and integrated townships. It is geographically diversified in Asia, with China, Singapore and Vietnam as its key markets, while it continues to scale up in India as well as other developed markets globally.

What sets us apart

Your needs shape our work

A big part of what we do is a result of listening and understanding what matters to you. That is why we're big on creating solutions that enhance your lifestyle and business.

Whether it’s building functional and quality homes and spaces, tapping on technology to create smart homes and offices or providing services that lighten your load, we aim to create vibrant and sustainable communities with our suite of offerings.

We build responsibly

We're focused on building a responsible and sustainable business, continually striving to create real estate solutions that mitigate climate change. We strive to build trust and confidence, focusing on high standards of corporate governance and transparency. These standards will continue to guide us as we expand our presence regionally.

We're with you every step of the way

Customer engagement and centricity define what we do. We understand that things like buying a home or leasing an office space are sometimes complex. You can count on us to be there, every step of the way.
