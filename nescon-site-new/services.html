Our Services

The scope of services offered by the company includes preparation of feasibility studies for various types of projects includes detailed analyses to establish technically sound project designs and their economic and financial viability. Economic perspective and the project objectives are defined and the different technical studies required are carried out. For selected projects designs, the economic and financial costs are compiled and compared with expected economic benefits. By considering economic benefits and economic costs, the economic viability is appraised. All these technical and economic studies are described in the project feasibility reports, submitted to the clients as the basic material for use by them to obtain project approval from the financing agencies concerned.

TOPOGRAPHIC SURVEYS & MAPPING

Topographic surveying is the process of determining the positions, both on plan and elevation, of the natural and artificial features of a locality for the purpose of delineating them by means of conventional signs upon a topographic map. By topography is meant the shape or configuration of the earth’s surface. The topographic surveys & mapping is considered an important element and is mainly used in almost every type of construction project. This can be used further in the master planning and cost estimation of different projects such as below.

  • New construction
  • Utility design
  • Roads or bridge design or improvements
  • Grading or drainage projects
  • Others

PROJECT MANAGEMENT

Project management is defined as the process of steering a project from the start through its lifecycle. The main objective of project management is to complete a project within the established goals of time, budget, and quality.

A project management life cycle starts when the project is initiated and ends when the project is completed. The project is defined by set of activities that uses resources (money, people, materials, energy, space, provisions, communication, quality, risk, etc.) to meet the predefined objectives.

Supervision means to watch the works or tasks and to ensure the quality control of the project as per specified standards. NESCON has highly qualified staff for managing and supervision of construction projects. In our firm for any construction project, the functions of project management includes the following:

  • Project objectives, specifications, plans and delineation of scope
  • Project budgeting, scheduling, setting performance requirements, and selecting project participants
  • Efficient and maximum resource utilization through procurement of labor, materials and equipment according to the prescribed schedule and plan.
  • Using proper coordination and control of planning, design, estimating, contracting and construction in the entire process.
  • Effective and efficient communications and mechanisms development for resolving conflicts among the various participants in the project.
