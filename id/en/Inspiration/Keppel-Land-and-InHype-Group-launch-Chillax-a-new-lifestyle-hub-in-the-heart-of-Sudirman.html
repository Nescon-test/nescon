Keppel Land and InHype Group launch Chillax, a new lifestyle hub in the heart of Sudirman

  1. Home
  2. Inspiration
  3. Keppel Land and InHype Group launch Chillax

Jakarta, 1 December 2022 – Keppel Land and InHype Group have officially launched Chillax, a 6,000-square metre lifestyle and placemaking commercial hub, in Jakarta, following the groundbreaking ceremony held in November 2021.

Strategically located in the heart of the Sudirman central business district, between International Financial Center Two and Chase Plaza Building, Chillax features more than 25 well-known tenants from various industries. These include food and beverage brands such as Gion Sushi, Butler’s Steak, TOMA Brasserie and Skinny Dip, as well as alfresco dining choices such as Hungryonuts, Smoked BBQ Lagi, Kwetiau Aho and Bakso Sultan, among others. Chillax also offers a variety of health and beauty, fashion and sports brands including Pinarello Bicycle, Giant Bicycle and Dance Station. With a 550-square metre function hall, Chillax also offers functional spaces where various events and activities can be held.  

 

Mr Allen Tan, President (Indonesia), Keppel Land, said, “Keppel Land seeks to deliver innovative urban space solutions that enrich people and communities. In line with this, we are pleased to announce the launch of Chillax with InHype group today. Chillax offers a refreshing lifestyle venue with a unique alfresco dining and placemaking retail concept – the first of its kind in Sudirman. We are confident that Chillax will inject a refreshing dose of vibrancy into the vicinity and become the preferred lifestyle destination for those working and living in the area.”

Mr Willy Kurniawan, CEO of InHype Group, said, “Food has the ability to bring people from all walks of life together. With a wide array of eateries and alfresco dining options offered at Chillax, it will serve as a place for people to connect and spend quality time with one another. We are pleased to work with Keppel Land on this contemporary retail concept and we will continue to work on creating exciting new experiences for our customers.” 

From 1 to 4 December 2022, Chillax, in collaboration with Semasa, a thematic bazaar organiser, will hold a creative market that features more than 80 local and regional brands. 

Open from 10am to 10pm daily, Chillax is located at Jalan Jenderal Sudirman Kav. 23 and is easily accessible via public transportation including the MRT and Transjakarta. For visitors taking the MRT, alight at Setiabudi Astra MRT Station and for those taking Transjakarta, alight at the Karet Stop. 

For more information about Chillax, visit https://www.instagram.com/chillax.jakarta/

About Keppel Land 

 

Keppel Land is a wholly-owned subsidiary of Keppel Corporation, a Singapore flagship multinational company providing solutions for sustainable urbanisation, focusing on energy & environment, urban development, connectivity and asset management.

Keppel Land is an innovative urban space solutions provider that leverages technology to deliver sustainable and customer-centric solutions that enrich people and communities. It is focused in the areas of sustainable urban renewal, senior living, urban living, retail and large-scale integrated developments.    

Its award-winning portfolio comprises investment-grade commercial properties, residential developments and integrated townships. It is geographically diversified in Asia, with China, Singapore and Vietnam as its key markets, while it continues to scale up in India as well as other developed markets globally.

 
About InHype Group

Since 2011, InHype Group has been focusing on food and beverage, hospitality, lifestyle, media and technology, and events planning. One of InHype’s key goals is to encourage active collaboration within communities through food, art, and lifestyle.

After launching the HYPE.ID Weekend Market at Pantai Indah Kapuk with great success, InHype Group is now adding new innovative projects to their portfolio, like the Coworking Space at Baymalk Mall Pluit and Jakarta Coffee Week, the largest coffee festival in Indonesia.

InHype Group will keep creating projects that have a "Positive Impact" and "Positive Lifestyle" for the community.
