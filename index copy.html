Welcome to NESCON

National Engineering Solutions & Consultants (NESCON) is an engineering consultant ﬁrm located in Peshawar, KPK and has sub ofﬁces in other parts of the country. The ﬁrm offer broad range of consulting services for construction, energy and development industry which are tailored to meet the needs of clients on an individual basis. With the help of highly qualiﬁed Professional Engineers and support staff; NESCON provide the level of expertise which is required to accomplish any challenging project in construction, energy and development sector.
